# Banking Policy Knowledge Base

This document contains official bank policies that should be referenced when responding to customer inquiries.

---

## Card Services

### POLICY-001: Card Replacement Timeline
Standard card replacements take 7-10 business days for delivery. Expedited delivery is available for a $25 fee with 2-3 business day delivery.

### POLICY-002: Lost/Stolen Card Reporting
Lost or stolen cards must be reported immediately. Customers are not liable for unauthorized transactions reported within 2 business days. Liability increases to $500 for reports made after 2 business days but within 60 days.

### POLICY-003: Card Transaction Disputes
Disputed charges must be reported within 60 days of the statement date. Investigation takes 10-45 business days depending on complexity. Provisional credit may be issued within 10 days for amounts over $50.

### POLICY-004: Daily Transaction Limits
Default daily ATM withdrawal limit is $500. Default daily debit card purchase limit is $2,500. Limits can be temporarily increased by calling customer service or through online banking.

---

## Account Management

### POLICY-005: Monthly Maintenance Fee Waiver
The $12 monthly maintenance fee can be waived by maintaining a minimum daily balance of $1,500, setting up direct deposit of $500+/month, or being enrolled in a student account program.

### POLICY-006: Account Fee Courtesy Waiver
Account fees (overdraft, maintenance, wire transfer) can be waived once per calendar year as a customer courtesy upon request, subject to account standing review.

### POLICY-007: Overdraft Protection Options
Customers can link a savings account for automatic transfers ($10 fee per transfer) or opt into overdraft coverage ($35 fee per item). Overdraft coverage can be declined at any time.

### POLICY-008: Account Closure Process
Accounts can be closed at any branch or by written request. Outstanding balances must be paid before closure. Remaining funds will be issued via check within 5-7 business days.

---

## Transfers and Payments

### POLICY-009: Wire Transfer Processing
Domestic wire transfers are processed same-day if submitted before 4 PM ET ($25 fee). International wires take 1-5 business days ($45 fee). Wire transfers cannot be cancelled once processed.

### POLICY-010: ACH Transfer Timing
ACH transfers between linked accounts take 1-3 business days. Same-day ACH is available for domestic transfers under $100,000 for an additional $5 fee.

### POLICY-011: Bill Pay Guarantee
If a bill payment arrives late due to our error and results in a late fee, we will cover the late fee up to $50 and send a letter to the payee on your behalf.

---

## Interest and Rates

### POLICY-012: Interest Rate Adjustments
Savings account interest rates are variable and may change at any time. Customers will be notified of rate changes at least 30 days in advance for decreases.

### POLICY-013: CD Early Withdrawal Penalty
Early withdrawal from a Certificate of Deposit incurs a penalty of 90 days' interest for terms under 12 months, or 180 days' interest for terms of 12 months or longer.

---

## Security and Fraud

### POLICY-014: Fraud Investigation Timeline
Fraud claims are investigated within 10 business days. For complex cases involving international transactions, investigation may take up to 45 days. Customers receive written notification of results.

### POLICY-015: Account Security Lockout
After 5 failed login attempts, online banking access is locked for 30 minutes. After 3 lockouts in 24 hours, customers must call to verify identity and reset access.

### POLICY-016: Two-Factor Authentication
Two-factor authentication is required for wire transfers over $1,000, adding new payees, and changing contact information. This cannot be disabled for security purposes.

---

## Customer Service

### POLICY-017: Response Time Commitment
Written complaints receive acknowledgment within 2 business days and full response within 15 business days. Complex issues may require up to 30 days with interim updates.

### POLICY-018: Branch Hours
Standard branch hours are Monday-Friday 9 AM - 5 PM, Saturday 9 AM - 1 PM. Extended hours available at select locations. All branches closed on federal banking holidays.

### POLICY-019: Account Statement Requests
Current statements are available free online. Paper statement reprints cost $5 each. Statements older than 7 years may require $25 research fee and 10-15 business days.

### POLICY-020: Power of Attorney Processing
Power of Attorney documents require notarized originals and 5-7 business days for legal review before account access is granted. Copies are not accepted.
